What is Pop it?

Pop it literally translates as pop, this is a children's game process in which it can be compared

with getting into an air-bubble wrapping film. But unlike a film, pop-its can be pressed

with characteristic sensations and sounds, endlessly dozens of silicone bubbles.

Rules of the game

Players take turns and click on the pimples. It is necessary to
finish the series that you started. You can press a maximum of 3
pimples at the same time (they must be nearby, you cannot press
several pimples at once if they are not right next to each other) The
task is to make the opponent go last. Who went last - he lost!
